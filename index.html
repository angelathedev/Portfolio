Introduction

Hello, I'm Angela McByrd! I am a current high school math teacher and aspiring software developer. I was born and raised in the beautiful, windy city of Chicago, IL. My determination, care, and creativity make me a special kind of mathematics educator.

About Me

After graduating college, I started teaching middle school science. I have always had a passion for computer science. I started an after school club teaching students to code using Codecademy and Code.org. I am excited for the opportunity to apply some of the principles and concepts I have used in the after-school program. I love solving problems and I love working in a team environment so I am excited for the opportunity to work in web development, providing clients with services they need! I have collaborated on curriculum in the past with my fellow educators. I loved having the opportunity to brainstorm ideas, learn from one another, and create new things. I hope to provide future clients with web applications that are tailored to their desires and needs, specifically focusing on educational technology web applications. I hope to also provide educators with detailed plans and a sense of assurance that they are receiving the best service with me.
